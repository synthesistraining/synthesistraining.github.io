Environmental Evidence is the official journal of the Collaboration for Environmental Evidence, which publishes protocols, reviews/maps, methods articles and commentaries.

The website of the Collaboration for Environmental Evidence (CEE) that coordinates and promotes high quality evidence synthesis.

The website of Cochrane, the systematic review coordinating body working in the field of healthcare.

The website of the Campbell Collaboration, the systematic review coordinating body working with social and economic interventions.

The Global Evidence Synthesis Initiative, whose goal is to enhance the capacity of Low- and Middle-Income Countries (LMICs) in synthesising evidence, and using synthesised evidence to support practice and policy across disciplines.

The GESI webinar series is a continuously updated set of Open Access webinars relating to evidence synthesis across disciplines.

The Systematic Review Toolbox is a continually updated database of tools and software for supporting systematic reviews.
